Nick Friesen

Founder of The Match Artist

As the founder of The Match Artist, Nick has built a $4M+ revenue company transforming dating profiles through high-end, natural photography. His expertise spans from traditional photography to cutting-edge AI, where he specializes in creating hyper-realistic headshots and branding content.

Beyond the tech world, Nick's passions run deep—from woodworking and welding to mastering the art of smoked barbecue. He's also a musician, finding time for piano and guitar between his creative pursuits.

Understanding the challenges of content creation firsthand, Nick focuses on making the process easier, more efficient, and authentically engaging. Whether through AI innovation, photography expertise, or hands-on production, he helps bring ideas to life without the typical stress and complexity.

Allie Friesen

Founder of The Wedding Artist

As the founder of The Wedding Artist, Allie has mastered the art of capturing timeless, authentic moments that tell a couple's unique story. Her passion for design runs deep, encompassing everything from graphic design and color theory to comprehensive brand development.

Currently, Allie is the creative force behind transforming their home into a multi-functional space for events and content creation. Every corner is thoughtfully designed for video shoots, photo sessions, and immersive experiences, combining beauty with purpose.

Her creative spirit extends to hands-on crafts like sewing and upholstery, where she breathes new life into vintage pieces. In everything she touches—from photography to space design—Allie brings an exceptional level of thoughtfulness and creativity.
